 *Master’s thesis in Cognitive Sciences - Pamela Castaneda*
## *How do variations in system transparency and decision contingencies interact to shape conditioned reliance on a Decision Support System?*

**Supervisor:** Arthur Prével
Université de Lille - Département de Psychologie de l’Université de Lille

The experiment is a simple image-based decision task using X-ray images of luggage. Participants must determine whether a dangerous object is present in each image.
The study investigates how AI assistance and AI transparency affect:
- decision performance
---
Total experiment duration:
≈ 60 minutes
Breakdown:
- Instructions + consent: 5–10 minutes
- Experimental task: 50–55 minutes

Total trials:
- Practice: 30
- Experimental blocks: 3 × 60

Total trials: 210
