How do variations in system transparency and
decision contingencies interact to shape condi-
tioned reliance on a Decision Support System?
Master’s thesis in Cognitive Sciences
Pamela Castaneda
Supervisor: Arthur Prével
2025 - 2026
Université de Lille
Département de Psychologie de l’Université de Lille

The experiment is a simple image-based decision task using X-ray images of luggage. Participants must determine whether a dangerous object is present in each image.
The study investigates how AI assistance and AI transparency affect:
- decision performance
- trust in automation
- perceived agency and responsibility
